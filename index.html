03

Where this
comes from.

The agenda refreshes itself every day from the Démosphère feed, and is completed by hand from the other sources below. Nothing here is invented: every event links back to where it was announced.

i

Démosphère Bruxelles

The city's activist agenda. Its public feed is pulled in automatically every morning, so recurring rallies and newly announced actions land here on their own.

bxl.demosphere.net
ii

YOUCA Protestagenda

A maintained list of protests and actions in Brussels and Flanders — added to the agenda by hand when relevant to Brussels.

youca.be
iii

City of Brussels

Official protest & traffic notices, published event by event. Also where you'll find the rules on demonstration permits.

brussels.be
04

The hardest
part is showing up.

Waypoint is a Brussels-based youth non-profit built around one idea: the biggest barrier to taking part in anything — an exchange abroad, a civic life, a demonstration — is not knowing where to start. This tracker applies that idea to the streets of Brussels: one clear agenda, so "I didn't know it was happening" stops being the reason you weren't there.

Waypoint was founded and is run by Francesco Serangeli.
