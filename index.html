02

The hardest
part is starting.

Waypoint helps students and student groups take part in Erasmus+ and other EU youth opportunities for the first time. It exists to dismantle the "I don't understand how this works, so I won't even try" barrier — guiding newcomers through their first international project and making European mobility reachable for people who'd otherwise count themselves out.
